How to Play

🍬 Swap adjacent candies to align 3 or more of the same color.

⚡ Match 4: Creates a Striped Candy (clears whole rows or columns).

📦 Match 5 in L/T: Creates a Wrapped Candy (explodes twice in a 3x3 grid).

🌈 Match 5 in a Line: Creates a Color Bomb. Swap it with any candy to clear all candies of that color!

🧼 Jelly Levels: Clear jelly by matching candies on top of them.

🚀 Combine special candies for massive explosions!
